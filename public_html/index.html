Intelligence Dashboard  ·  Signal-First News

Project RollUp is a real-time news intelligence dashboard. It continuously monitors 40+ global sources — wire services, investigative outlets, science journals, and more — and surfaces the stories that matter most.

Every headline is automatically clustered and ranked based on freshness, how many independent outlets are covering it, and editorial velocity. The higher the rank, the stronger the signal.

Click OPEN BRIEFING on any story to read a fact-based write-up, see which other outlets are corroborating the story, and view a live globe showing where the source outlet is based.
